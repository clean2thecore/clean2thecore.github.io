From sand box relics to full size vehicles Our Story

As even a young man, our owner Cory enjoyed cleaning and restoring things on wheels. Anything from ‘Tonka’ trucks to mountain bikes, if it had wheels and got dirty, it got thoroughly cleaned and shined afterwards.

‘Perhaps it’s something instilled in me through my mother, always instructing to take great care of anything I was given or spent my hard earned money on. Things that are well taken care of, last.’ - Cory


As he’s grown, the enjoyment of making vehicles shine like new has transitioned from ‘Tonka’ trucks to actual vehicles. With a growing desire to care for not only vehicles of family, ‘Clean To The CorE’ was opened to allow for a much broader clientele of vehicles to care for like his own.

‘The enjoyment of driving your vehicle that can be renewed after a thorough cleaning is quite amazing to feel, time and time again. That’s what I’m trying to convey to you through my service.’ - Cory
